**Celestia Island** is an independent open-source organization exploring the frontiers of systems engineering.

The name draws from the floating island in the sky — a symbol of transcending boundaries. Our credo, **"Tech otakus save the world,"** reflects a belief that dedicated, deeply skilled engineers can drive meaningful change through open collaboration.

Each project is crafted with care, pushing the boundaries of what modern systems software can achieve.

Though our projects span different domains, they share a common design philosophy: Rust-first architecture, modular composability, and engineering pragmatism over academic perfection. From low-level frameworks to end-user applications, we are building a cohesive ecosystem piece by piece.
